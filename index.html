About Net-Recon

Leadership

David Godwin

Founder & Managing Partner

David is a technologist and entrepreneur with more than 25 years of experience productizing enterprise-level solutions. He is the Co-founder, President and Chairman of the Board at Slingshot Aerospace and a partner at ATX Seed Ventures.

A seasoned startup advisor, David has guided several businesses to product marketability and has had multiple successful exits from venture-backed companies. He specializes in architecting cloud-driven applications, data platforms, and analytics solutions.

David is from Texas and is active in his community, having served on several non-profit boards in Austin including Heart House Austin and the Star of Texas Fair and Rodeo.

Based in Austin, Texas

Net-Recon is headquartered in Austin — one of America's fastest-growing technology hubs. Austin's vibrant startup ecosystem, world-class universities, and culture of innovation make it the ideal home for a software venture firm.

The city's deep talent pool in software engineering, data science, and product design — combined with Texas's business-friendly environment — gives our portfolio companies access to the resources they need to scale.
